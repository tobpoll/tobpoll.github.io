Toby is a designer and researcher based in London. His work explores the ways in which the modern world has shifted understandings of reality. Working across areas spanning the development of AI, to the rise of far-right politics; Toby’s work employs design methodologies to shed light on the fuzzy ontologies of a rapidly changing world.
